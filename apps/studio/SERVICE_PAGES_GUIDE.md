## How to Create in Sanity Studio

1. Go to <http://localhost:3335/structure/service>
2. Click "Create new Service"
3. Copy and paste the content from above
4. Fill in all fields
5. Click "Publish"
6. Repeat for each service

Once published, these will automatically appear on your services page and can be linked from the main navigation menu.
